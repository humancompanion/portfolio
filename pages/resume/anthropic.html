Profile

I've spent the last decade inside the federal government — at the VA, U.S. Digital Service, and OPM — helping agencies adopt technology that measurably improves how they serve the public. I know how government funds, governs, and adopts software because I've done it from the inside: co-authoring a $7.4M Technology Modernization Fund investment, running a governance program supporting 60+ product teams, and leading a cross-functional initiative under Congressional scrutiny.

For the past three years I've focused on AI adoption in government — building an open-source MCP server and prototyping toolchain, training and supporting the designers who use them, and quantifying the results: an 80% reduction in cost per digitized form and thousands of years of Veteran burden time saved. I'm a technical practitioner who can explain AI to any audience, and I want to help public sector agencies get real mission value from Claude.
